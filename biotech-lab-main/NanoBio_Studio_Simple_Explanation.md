# NanoBio Studio — What Does This Technology Do?

## Simple One-Page Explanation

**NanoBio Studio** is a web-based interactive platform that helps people understand, design, and test nanoparticle-based medical solutions—without needing a physical laboratory.

In simple terms, it lets users build a virtual nanoparticle, see how it behaves inside the body, estimate its safety and cost, and generate an experimental plan, all in one place.

---

## 🧠 What problem does it solve?

Designing nanomedicine is complex. Scientists must balance:
- particle size and charge
- biological targeting
- how the drug moves in the body
- safety risks
- production cost

Normally, learning or testing these ideas requires advanced labs, long timelines, and high budgets. **NanoBio Studio turns this into a clear, visual, and interactive process** that anyone can explore safely and quickly.

---

## 🔬 What does the user actually do?

Inside NanoBio Studio, a user can:

### 1. Choose or design a nanoparticle
Adjust size, surface charge, coating (ligands), and drug payload using sliders and menus.

### 2. Match it with a biological target
For example: tumor tissue, blood plasma, or a specific cell type.

### 3. Simulate drug delivery in the body
The app shows simple graphs of how the drug moves between blood and tissue over time (a simplified PK/PD model).

### 4. Check safety and toxicity risk
The system gives a risk score based on known scientific rules (size, dose, charge, aggregation, etc.).

### 5. Estimate cost
Users see how design choices affect manufacturing cost per batch or per patient.

### 6. Generate an experimental protocol
The platform automatically creates a step-by-step lab-style plan that can be downloaded and used for teaching or planning.

## 🎓 Who is it for?

NanoBio Studio is designed for:

No advanced coding or installation is required—everything runs in a browser.
